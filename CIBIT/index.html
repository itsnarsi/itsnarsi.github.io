ABOUT

Computational Intelligence and Bio-Identification Technologies Lab (CIBIT) is a part of Computer Science and Electrical Engineering Department, School of Computing and Engineering, University of Missouri-Kansas City.

Our research group conducts research in biomedical signal analysis, biometrics (physical and psychophysiological), and physiological system identification using computational intelligence paradigms such as artificial neural networks. Some recent projects include noninvasive brain computer interfacing, a new patented biometric modality based on vasculature on the white of the eye, data-driven modeling of human joints, and non-obtrusive psychophysiological pattern recognition using postural and ocular dynamics.
